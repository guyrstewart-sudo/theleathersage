The Maker

Made by hand,
given with heart.

I'm Sage Holmes, the hands behind The Leather Sage. From a small studio in Asheville, I cut, tool, stitch, and set every piece myself — leather and gemstone becoming something you can wear.

My work is drawn from the natural world: leaf, stone, river, and bone. Each adornment is one of a kind and made slowly, on a custom list, one piece at a time. Nothing leaves my hands without love in it.

— Sage
